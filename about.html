About Barangay Poblacion

A brief history, our mission, community profile, and the services we provide to residents and visitors.

History

Barangay Poblacion (Legleg) is a community in Quirino, Ilocos Sur with a long history of farming, community cooperation and cultural traditions. The barangay has evolved from a small agricultural settlement into a hub for local artisans and ecotourism. Local families maintain rice terraces and traditional crafts passed down through generations.

Vision & Mission

Vision: A resilient, prosperous and culturally vibrant Poblacion where residents enjoy improved quality of life while preserving our natural and cultural heritage.

Mission: To deliver transparent local governance and community programs that support sustainable agriculture, responsible tourism, public safety, and access to basic services.

Core Values

  • Community first — participation and cooperation
  • Stewardship — protection of natural and cultural resources
  • Transparency — accountable local governance
  • Inclusivity — services for all residents

Where We Are

Barangay Poblacion is located in Legleg, Quirino, Ilocos Sur. We are part of the municipal area and reachable via provincial transport from Vigan and nearby towns.

  • Provincial capital: Vigan — approx. 1 hr by road
  • Local transport: jeepneys and habal-habal
