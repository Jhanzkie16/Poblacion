Visitor Information

Plan your visit: responsible travel tips, safety, and local guidelines for visitors to Barangay Poblacion.

  • Getting there: From Vigan, take the provincial bus to Quirino; jeepneys and habal-habal connect to Legleg. Travel time: ~1–1.5 hours depending on connections.
  • Permits & fees: Most attractions are free; special guided activities or events may have small fees. Check with the barangay office for group visits.
  • When to go: Dry season (Nov—Apr) is recommended for hikes and photography. Expect occasional afternoon rains in the wet season.
  • Stay & Eat: Basic homestays and guesthouses in Legleg; support local eateries for authentic meals.
